Product Development

Momma’s Pop Shop LLC is actively developing a new line of Modular Botanical Workstation Systems designed for small‑scale botanical processing and preparation workflows. These systems focus on organization, ergonomics, and modularity — allowing users to configure their workspace for trimming, sorting, grinding, and other botanical tasks.

Prototyping & Capabilities

3D Printing

In‑house prototyping using a Bambu Lab P1S enables rapid iteration, small‑batch component production, and early functional testing.

CAD Modeling

Over a decade of experience in SolidWorks, including sheet‑metal design, assemblies, and mechanical drafting.

Engineering Background

12+ years in process engineering, electronics manufacturing, and fabrication environments — now applied to building our own OEM products.

Our Mission

We are an early‑stage hardware startup focused on designing practical, modular workstation solutions for botanical preparation. Our goal is to move from 3D‑printed prototypes to scalable manufacturing methods such as injection molding or sheet‑metal fabrication as the product line matures.

To support development during this early phase, we also operate an online resale shop — helping fund prototyping, materials, and future production.
