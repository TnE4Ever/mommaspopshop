Who I Am

The Person Behind Momma’s Pop Shop

I worked my way through school doing custom framing — learning how to measure twice, cut once, and care about the details people actually see every day.

From there, I spent years working in a not‑for‑profit insurance program that helps people who struggle to get coverage in the standard market. We do the work of a full insurance company with a lean team, which means staying organized, focused, and efficient every single day.

Along the way, I earned a CPCU (Chartered Property Casualty Underwriter) and later moved into accounting work. That mix of craftsmanship, financial discipline, and serving people who don’t always get the easy version of life is what I bring into this business. I care about details, I care about numbers, and I care about building products that make people’s day‑to‑day lives easier.

Behind the scenes, the business draws on over a decade of hands-on engineering and manufacturing experience — electronics assembly, process engineering, quality improvement, and fabrication. That includes IPC‑A‑610 and IPC‑7711/21 certifications for assembly and rework. It’s real production-floor experience, and it drives the way every product here is designed, built, and evaluated.
