01 — Product & Ecosystem
POP Benchline System

The Platform, The First Layout, And Everything That Comes After

The POP Benchline System is a modular platform for small‑scale botanical work, electronics, and mixed‑use benches. The first layout on that platform is the POP Top — a compact, defined workspace that keeps your tools and containers in their lane.

From there, the plan is simple: more layouts, more modules, and more ways to make a folding table feel like a purpose‑built workstation.

The details live on their own pages — product, engineering, concepts, materials, and more — but it all starts here.

If you want the deep dive on the POP Top itself, head to product.html. If you want to see how the whole Benchline ecosystem fits together, go to benchline-series.html.

02 — Who’s Behind This
Story Preview

Not A Faceless Brand

Momma’s Pop Shop is small on purpose — built by someone who’s spent years in the real world of work: custom framing, not‑for‑profit insurance, accounting, and hands‑on engineering and manufacturing.

The business draws on over a decade of production‑floor experience: electronics assembly, process engineering, quality improvement, and fabrication, backed by IPC‑A‑610 and IPC‑7711/21 certifications.

If you want the full story — how those worlds connect and why this shop exists — that lives on story.html.
