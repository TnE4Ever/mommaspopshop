Product Line

Modular Botanical Workstation Systems

Built for small‑scale botanical prep—rolling, sorting, trimming, and storing—without the usual mess and wasted space.

We’re designing workstation setups that give everything a place: tools, materials, and surfaces that actually work together. The idea is simple—keep the work area clean, keep what you need within reach, and let the layout adapt as habits and setups change.

These systems are meant to feel like something you want to use every day, not just something that looks good in a photo.

How We Build

Real‑World Know‑How Behind the Bench

Our development work is supported by access to real‑world engineering experience, including more than a decade working under IPC‑A‑610 and IPC‑7711/21 standards. That background shapes how we think about fit, repeatability, and clean, dependable design instead of just “good enough.”

We use proven CAD workflows and in‑house 3D printing to move from idea to physical parts quickly, so layouts can be tested, adjusted, and tested again until they feel right in actual use.

Who We Are

A Small Shop With Serious Backbone

Momma’s Pop Shop is owned and led by someone who has spent years doing the kind of work that doesn’t make headlines but keeps things standing: custom framing at Jo‑Ann Fabrics, earning a degree in Business Finance, then a bachelor’s in Accounting, and building a 15‑year career inside a single insurance company.

She became the youngest and fastest in her department to earn a CPCU in underwriting, then stepped into accounting when the company needed it and stayed there to keep the numbers straight. That mix of craftsmanship, finance, and long‑term commitment is exactly what shows up here.

The shop is run with the same mindset: respect the details, know your numbers, and don’t cut corners just because nobody’s looking.

How That Helps This Business

Her background keeps the business side sharp—pricing, risk, and cash flow are handled with the same care as the products.
