Product Line

Modular Botanical Workstation Systems

Built for sorting, trimming, organizing, and storing plant material without the usual mess and clutter.

We are developing workstation setups that give everything a place: tools, containers, and surfaces that work together. The goal is simple—keep the work area clean, keep what you need close, and let the layout change as your setup changes.

These systems are meant to feel natural to use, not confusing or over‑designed.

How We Build

Practical Design With Real Experience Behind It

Our product development is supported by access to real‑world engineering experience, including more than ten years working under electronics manufacturing standards like IPC‑A‑610 and IPC‑7711/21. These standards focus on quality, clean work, and doing things the right way, and that mindset guides how we design our parts.

We use proven CAD tools and in‑house 3D printing to move from idea to real parts, test them, adjust them, and improve them.

Design

Who I Am

The Person Behind Momma’s Pop Shop

I worked my way through school doing custom framing, then built a long‑term career in insurance. Along the way, I earned a CPCU (Chartered Property Casualty Underwriter), which is a high‑level insurance and risk designation. Later, I moved into accounting work inside the same company.

That mix of craftsmanship, money management, and long‑term commitment is what I bring into this business. I care about details, I care about numbers, and I care about making things that actually help people in their day‑to‑day lives.

How That Shapes This Business

I handle the business, the books, and the quality standards. Product development is supported by access to engineering and manufacturing experience, so ideas can move from sketches to real, testable hardware. Together, that lets us build products that are practical, reliable, and easy to live with.
