Who We Are

Small Shop, Serious Background

Momma’s Pop Shop is a family‑run business based in Columbus, Ohio. The company is owned and led by someone who has spent years balancing real‑world work and education—custom framing at Jo‑Ann Fabrics, earning a degree in Business Finance from Columbus State, then a bachelor’s in Accounting from Franklin University.

For over 15 years, that same drive has carried into the insurance world—earning a CPCU in underwriting faster than anyone else in her department, then stepping into accounting when the company needed it and staying there to keep things running smoothly.

That mix of hands‑on work, financial discipline, and long‑term commitment is the backbone of this business.

How That Shows Up Here

The shop is run with the same mindset: know your numbers, respect the details, and build things that last. Product development is supported by access to engineering and manufacturing know‑how, while the business side is grounded in real accounting and risk experience.
